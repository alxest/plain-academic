Hi! I am Youngju Song, a postdoc working with Derek Dreyer at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems.

My research interest lie in formally verfiying realistic low-level systems. To tackle this problem, I am especially interested in three directions: (i) developing a unified theory that fuses benefits of different existing techniques like separation logic and refinement, (ii) formalizing advanced features like casting between integers and pointers, undefined values and undefined behaviors, and (iii) making formal verification less expensive, by giving more ergonomic interface and employing more automation. Most of my previous and ongoing research falls into these categories.

I obtained my Bachelor's degree at KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science & Technology) and my Ph.D degree at Seoul National University.

I will be on the job market this fall (2023)!
